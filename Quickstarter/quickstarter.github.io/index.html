The Story

The idea for Quickstarter was simple: to give beginners everything they need to enter the world of programming. We aim to make complex concepts easy to understand at the most basic level, so that you can move on into more detailed applications. Quickstarter's first product is CodeByExamples, and we plan to develop many more products keeping the same principle in mind. At Quickstarter, we will provide you the impetus to dive deep into the subject of your choice, and to do it in as little time as possible.
